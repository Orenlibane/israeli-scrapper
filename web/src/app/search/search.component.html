Nadlan Scout

Real-time Yad2 market intelligence — find undervalued apartments before everyone else.

Where does the data come from?

All listings are scraped directly from Yad2 using their pagination API — not screenshots or third-party feeds. 18 major cities are scanned daily at 06:00 AM Israel time.

  • Up to 40 pages per city, per deal type
  • Private owner + agency listings
  • Price changes tracked over time
  • Listings marked inactive after 48h off-market

How does the comparison engine work?

After each scan, the engine groups listings by city + deal type + room count and computes a market benchmark from sold transactions (nadlan.gov.il) when available:

  1. Peer grouping — e.g. all 3-room sale apartments in Ashdod
  2. Outlier trimming — removes top and bottom 10% by ₪/m²
  3. Median benchmark — sold prices preferred; active listing median as fallback
  4. Classification — each listing is scored vs. the benchmark
DealMore than 15% below median
Below5–15% below median
FairWithin ±5% of median
HighMore than 5% above median

How to use the app

  1. Search — pick a city, set filters, click Scan Yad2 for fresh data or Load Results from DB.
  2. Opportunities — click Run Comparison Engine, browse deals sorted by biggest discount.
  3. Share — every listing has a WhatsApp share button. Send deals directly to a group or contact.
  4. City chips — switch markets instantly without re-opening the dropdown.
